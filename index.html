I like building web, mobile and applied AI systems that are useful in production.

2025 -

I am currently a Senior Applied AI Architect at Indian River State College, building applied AI systems for students, advisors, staff, and faculty.

My first project was River Guide. I thought I was building a chatbot, but the real product became a truth layer:

  1. hard facts in a database
  2. explanations in a small wiki
  3. tools and evals that keep the model from guessing

It is now used by hundreds of students, advisors, staff, and faculty daily.

2024 -
JC

I started doing freelance work and quickly learned what it feels like to be brought in on day one and trusted with the whole stack. Some of the work was frontend, but a lot of it was backend and infrastructure: AWS, deployments, integrations, and whatever else had to exist for the site to launch.

The surreal version was showcase work for Stellantis, Jeep, Dodge, Chrysler, and Fiat: a new vehicle, a new collaboration, and a website that had to make it real.

2022 - 2024

At Modea, I owned the patient-facing mobile app work for Ballad Health.

Healthcare software was quieter than startup work, but harder in a different way: the app had to fit Epic/MyChart constraints, patient expectations, client expectations, and the release process of a real health system. This chapter taught me that the technical answer is only half the job; the other half is turning expectation and reality into something shippable.

2019 - 2022

At FloSports, I worked on the data platform behind its live sports streaming products.

I built backend services that moved sports data from live events into stats, rankings, team pages, and player profiles across multiple sport verticals. This work ran in production and was seen by millions of fans.

2018 - 2019

FanHero was a small startup, and it was where my engineering skills got sharpened by pressure. There were plenty of 3am nights, and a few nights where the office beanbag chair became the bed.

In the end, I helped build the mobile app for Orlando City SC, used by thousands of fans to follow the club, watch video, and stay connected.

bio

He is currently a Senior Applied AI Architect at Indian River State College, where he builds grounded AI systems like River Guide: assistants that use tools, evals, and a truth layer instead of guessing.
